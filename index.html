About Our CF Studio

Since our humble inception in 1971, we have focused on the core elements of Chamunda Furnishers which are Style, Comfort, Quality, Flexibility & Craftsmanship.

Our beautifully crafted work appeal to a wide section of customers. We give them the freedom to create looks & levels of comfort, that speak to their individual taste & lifestlyes.

Our Portfolio of work is diverse & involves working with Archietects, Interior Designers, &/or directly with the client-crousel to provide Stylish, Funcitional, Quality Furniture.
